## Contents of Raw Data Archive

### geodatabase/
- `MyProject.gdb.zip` — Full ArcGIS geodatabase with all final analysis layers
- `GTFS_Database.gdb.zip` — GTFS feature classes clipped to Baltimore City

### gtfs_raw/
- Original MTA Maryland GTFS `.txt` feeds (stops.txt, stop_times.txt, routes.txt, trips.txt, calendar.txt)
- Downloaded from: feeds.mta.maryland.gov
